Good Questions

Frequently Asked

Q. Is the consultation really free?

Yes — completely free and no-obligation. You'll get a cash offer and a listing estimate to compare, whether or not you decide to sell with us.

Q. Which is better — cash or listing?

It depends on your goals. Cash wins on speed and certainty; listing usually nets more money. We'll show you the real numbers for both so you can choose.

Q. Do you charge for staging?

When you list with us, professional staging and design guidance from Beck & Co. is built into our service to help your home sell for more.

Q. What if my house needs a lot of work?

No problem. We buy as-is for cash, or we can guide the specific repairs worth doing before a listing. Your call.

Q. Am I obligated to sell if I book?

Not at all. Many people book just to understand their options. There's never any pressure.
