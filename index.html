Good Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How fast can you buy my house for cash in Jacksonville?

We can close in as little as 7 days with a cash offer, or on any later date you choose. There are no banks or financing to fall through.

Q. Is the consultation and cash offer really free?

Yes — completely free and no-obligation. You'll get a cash offer and a listing estimate to compare, whether or not you decide to sell with us.

Q. Should I sell for cash or list on the market?

It depends on your goals. Cash wins on speed and certainty; listing usually nets more money. As licensed Realtors and cash buyers, we show you the real numbers for both so you can choose.

Q. Do you buy houses that need repairs?

Yes. We buy homes fully as-is for cash in any condition — including houses needing major repairs. Or we can guide the specific repairs worth doing before a listing.

Q. What areas do you serve?

Jacksonville and Duval County, St. Augustine and St. Johns County, and Clay County including Orange Park, Fleming Island and Middleburg — plus Ponte Vedra and the greater First Coast.

Q. Am I obligated to sell if I request an offer?

Not at all. Many homeowners reach out just to understand their options. There's never any pressure.
